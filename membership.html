For undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate students interested in bioscience, biomedical research, microbiology, biotechnology, or public health.

Professional Member

For researchers, clinicians, academics, laboratory professionals, biotechnology professionals, and public health workers.

For senior professionals and supporters who wish to contribute to the long-term development of BBHS.
