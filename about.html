About Bangladesh Bio Health Society

A simple society website for bio-health research, education, communication, and collaboration.

Who we are

A professional platform for bio-health science

Bangladesh Bio Health Society is developed as a science-focused platform for people interested in microbiology, biomedical science, biotechnology, pharmacy, antimicrobial resistance, One Health, public health, and related fields.

The aim is to keep the society work simple and useful: membership, educational resources, events, research communication, and networking.

Core aims
  • Support students and early-career researchers
  • Promote responsible scientific communication
  • Share useful academic and public-health resources
  • Encourage collaboration among bio-health professionals
  • Organize seminars, workshops, and member activities
Focus areas

Scientific scope

These areas can be changed or expanded based on the society decision.

Microbiology and AMR

Microbial systems, infection biology, antimicrobial resistance, bacteriophages, and microbial ecology.

Biomedical science

Host-microbe interactions, inflammation, disease models, diagnostics, and translational research.

Public health and One Health

Health awareness, environment, food safety, zoonosis, and community-based education.
