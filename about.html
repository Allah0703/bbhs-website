About BBHS

A professional society for bio-health science

Bangladesh Bio Health Society is a scientific platform dedicated to strengthening bioscience, biomedical research, biotechnology, microbiology, public health, and healthcare innovation in Bangladesh.

Who we are

BBHS is being developed as an inclusive professional society for researchers, clinicians, academics, students, laboratory professionals, public health workers, and other bio-health professionals.

The society aims to support scientific exchange, professional development, evidence-based discussion, and collaboration among people working in life science and health-related disciplines.

Core values

  • Scientific integrity and transparency.
  • Education, mentorship, and capacity building.
  • Collaboration across disciplines and institutions.
  • Evidence-based public health communication.
  • Support for young researchers and students.
Mission and vision

Building a connected bio-health community

BBHS will work to create opportunities for training, scientific meetings, professional networking, and collaborative research communication.

Mission

To promote bioscience and health-related research, education, and public health awareness through collaboration and professional engagement.

Vision

To become a recognized scientific society connecting Bangladeshi bio-health professionals locally and internationally.

Approach

Seminars, workshops, conferences, mentorship, educational resources, public health activities, and research communication.
