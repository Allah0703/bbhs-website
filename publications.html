News & Updates

This section will be used for official BBHS announcements, program updates, workshop notices, event reports, member achievements, and society-related scientific news.

Only verified society information should be posted here. Public health updates should be written carefully and should not replace professional medical advice.

Educational Resources

BBHS may prepare and share educational materials for students, researchers, and public health professionals. Possible materials include scientific writing guides, workshop slides, research method notes, public health awareness sheets, and curated reading lists.

Members can also use the dedicated Resources page for links to PubMed, PMC, WHO, CDC, NIH, and other open educational sources.

Microbial Systems Journal

Microbial Systems is a scientific publication platform covering microbiology, microbial ecology, antimicrobial resistance, host–microbe interactions, environmental microbiology, and biomedical microbial research.

If formally approved, Microbial Systems may be linked with BBHS as an associated publication or scientific communication platform. The relationship should be clearly defined by the society committee before using terms such as “official journal” or “affiliated journal.”

Suggested wording for now: “Microbial Systems may serve as an associated scientific publication and communication platform after formal approval by the BBHS committee.”
