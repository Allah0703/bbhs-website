Bangladesh Bio Health Society (BBHS) is a professional platform for researchers, clinicians, academics, students, and health professionals working across bioscience, biomedical research, biotechnology, microbiology, and public health.

About BBHS

A professional home for bio-health science

Bangladesh Bio Health Society is being developed as an inclusive scientific society dedicated to strengthening bioscience, biomedical research, health innovation, and evidence-based public health activities in Bangladesh.

The society aims to build a bridge among universities, research institutes, healthcare professionals, early-career scientists, and students through seminars, workshops, training programs, scientific meetings, and collaborative initiatives.

Focus Areas

What we work on

Bioscience & Biotechnology

Supporting education, research exchange, and innovation in biological and biotechnology-related fields.

Biomedical Research

Encouraging research on disease mechanisms, diagnostics, therapeutics, antimicrobial resistance, and translational health science.

Microbiology & One Health

Promoting work on microbes, antimicrobial resistance, host–microbe interactions, environmental health, and One Health approaches.

Public Health

Supporting awareness, training, and evidence-based discussion on health challenges relevant to Bangladesh.
