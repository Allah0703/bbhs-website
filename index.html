About BBHS

Built for responsible science communication

Bangladesh Bio Health Society aims to support education, research communication, and collaboration in the broad field of bio-health science.

  • Microbiology, biotechnology, biomedical science, pharmacy, and public health
  • Student and early-career researcher support
  • Workshops, seminars, and member networking
  • Open educational resources and scientific awareness
