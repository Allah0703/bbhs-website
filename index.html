Society overview

A professional platform for science, health, and collaboration

Bangladesh Bio Health Society is being developed as an inclusive professional society for researchers, clinicians, academics, students, laboratory professionals, biotechnology professionals, and public health workers.

The society will support scientific meetings, research training, educational resources, public health awareness, and links to trustworthy biomedical information.
