Bangladesh Bio Health Society

A professional society for students, researchers, clinicians, pharmacists, laboratory professionals, and public health workers interested in microbiology, biomedical science, pharmacy, biotechnology, and One Health.

About

Bangladesh Bio Health Society is a non-profit scientific and educational platform created to support bio-health science communication, academic collaboration, professional networking, and public health awareness. The society welcomes members from microbiology, biomedical science, pharmacy, biotechnology, medicine, veterinary science, environmental health, nutrition, laboratory science, and related fields.

The main aim of BBHS is to bring together Bangladeshi and international professionals who want to share knowledge, develop research capacity, support young scientists, and promote responsible scientific discussion. The society may organize seminars, training sessions, conference activities, research discussions, journal clubs, and public awareness programs.

BBHS will continue to develop its committee, membership system, conference platform, and publication activities step by step. Some sections of the website are intentionally kept simple so that names, dates, notices, and official details can be updated easily later.
