Our purpose

To create an inclusive scientific society that promotes research, education, collaboration, and public health awareness in Bangladesh.

  • Promote scientific collaboration among researchers and institutions.
  • Support research training, seminars, workshops, and scientific meetings.
  • Encourage evidence-based public health discussion and awareness.
  • Connect students, early-career scientists, clinicians, and professionals.

A platform for science, health, and collaboration

Bangladesh Bio Health Society is being developed as a professional forum for people working in bioscience, biomedical research, biotechnology, microbiology, healthcare, and public health.

The society seeks to build bridges among universities, research institutes, healthcare professionals, students, and early-career scientists through scientific exchange, training, and collaborative initiatives.

Focus areas

What we work on

BBHS supports scientific exchange and professional development across key bio-health disciplines relevant to Bangladesh and the global scientific community.

Bioscience & Biotechnology

Supporting education, research exchange, and innovation in biological and biotechnology-related fields.

Biomedical Research

Encouraging work on disease mechanisms, diagnostics, therapeutics, antimicrobial resistance, and translational health science.

Microbiology & One Health

Promoting work on microbes, host–microbe interactions, environmental health, antimicrobial resistance, and One Health approaches.

Public Health

Supporting awareness, training, and evidence-based discussion on health challenges relevant to Bangladesh.

Training & Mentorship

Creating opportunities for students and early-career researchers through seminars, workshops, and mentorship activities.

Scientific Communication

Encouraging clear communication of scientific evidence through meetings, newsletters, publications, and outreach.
