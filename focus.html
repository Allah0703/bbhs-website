Focus Areas

Scientific areas supported by BBHS

BBHS supports scientific exchange and professional development across key bio-health disciplines relevant to Bangladesh and the global scientific community.

Bioscience & Biotechnology

Education, research exchange, and innovation in biological science, biotechnology, molecular biology, and related fields.

Biomedical Research

Disease mechanisms, diagnostics, therapeutics, translational health science, and preclinical research communication.

Microbiology & One Health

Microbes, antimicrobial resistance, host–microbe interactions, environmental health, and One Health approaches.

Public Health

Evidence-based discussion and training on health challenges relevant to Bangladesh and low-resource settings.

Training & Mentorship

Scientific writing, data presentation, research methods, career guidance, and support for early-career researchers.

Scientific Communication

Meetings, reports, educational resources, newsletters, and professional communication of scientific evidence.
