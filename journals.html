Microbial Systems

Microbial Systems

Microbial Systems is a scientific publication platform focused on microbiology, microbial ecology, antimicrobial resistance, bacteriophages, host-microbe interactions, microbial biotechnology, gut microbiome research, environmental microbiology, microbial genomics, and applied microbiology.

The journal welcomes original research, review articles, short reviews, short communications, methods papers, and educational scientific articles after editorial assessment and peer review.
