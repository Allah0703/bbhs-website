Active / associated platform
Microbial Systems

Microbial Systems

Microbial Systems is a scientific publication platform focused on microbiology, microbial ecology, host-microbe interaction, antimicrobial resistance, bacteriophages, microbial biotechnology, environmental microbiology, gut microbiome research, and related areas.

The journal can publish original research, reviews, short reviews, short communications, methods, and educational scientific articles after editorial review. Its scope is suitable for studies involving bacteria, fungi, viruses, phages, microbial communities, bioactive compounds, microbial genomics, and applied microbiology.

Official indexing, DOI registration, editorial board details, and publication policies should be updated step by step as the journal develops.
